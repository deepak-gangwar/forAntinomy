Process

What we do?

Art Direction

The process starts with briefing and stating the project
goals and then working on the visual narrative to make our
work bold, beauriful and functtional. This is the stage where
we decide on the overall feel of the project including some
typography, initial colors and other elements that we need
to work with.

Design

My ultimate goal with every project is to come up with a
solution-based design approach to help my clients solve
real cases and achieve business needs.

We start with low fidelity prototypes that focus more on
functionality and problem solving and then adding onto it
more visuals and design as we progress.

Development

This stage involves bringing the project to life. This involves
coding the website and analyzing its preformance because
in this attention deficit world, split seconds make the
difference. We focus on clean and semantic code that is
easier for other people and Google to work with .
